# MRR Tracker Product Register

## Product Position

[DECISION] MRR Tracker is a weekly founder review tool for Stripe-backed solo SaaS businesses that need new, expansion, contraction, and churn changes explained before Monday planning.

## Audience

[DECISION] The first audience is solo founders with one Stripe account, fewer than 500 active subscriptions, and no finance analyst.

## Promise

[DECISION] MRR Tracker turns Stripe subscription movement into a five-minute Monday operating review with the exact accounts behind each change.
